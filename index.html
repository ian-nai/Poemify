Poemify

This page transforms the text in the text area below into lines of pseudo-random length, using a hidden Markov model to sequence the words by parts of speech. The text generation algorithm is based off of this example from @shiffman.

Your poemified text will appear in both the text box to the left and the empty paragraph to the right. The lines on the right are draggable, and the text on the left is fully editable.
